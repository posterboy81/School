How to Lunge and Strike Your Opponent according to Maestro Ridolfo Capoferro

Menu: Ridolfo Capoferro En Guardia! Lunge!

Ridolfo Capoferro was a fencing master in 17th Century Italy. His fencing manual, Gran Simulacro dell'Arte e dell'Uso della Scherma, or Great Representation of the Art and Use of Fencing was published in 1610 and is one of the foundational texts of what we now refer to as the Italian Tradition of fencing.

Italian fencing is a nuanced martial art, one that has a great depth and breadth of skills and knowledge that one must understand in order to master, however for the purposes of this exercise we are going to look at the most basic stance and movement: the guard position and the lunge.

If you'd like to know more about Capoferro you can read about him at his Wikipedia page, and more information on the Gran Simulacro is available WMA Wiki.
