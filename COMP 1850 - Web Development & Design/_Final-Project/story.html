The Brunch Club Story

Brunch Club was founded in June 2007 as four friends met at a local brunch spot for one of their birthdays. About halfway through that meal someone said "this is nice, we should do this again."

That fateful sentence was met with another "How about next week?". The natural follow up was "How about every week."

No one knows who said what, or who the one person who remained silent was, those details have since passed into myth and legend. What we do know is that since that fateful day Brunch Club has met every weekend without fail. That's 12 years of Saturdays as of June 2019.

VanCity Brunch Club is a private social club.
