Welcome to the homepage of the VanCity Brunch Club, a private social club that meets once per week, every week, and enjoys brunch together.

Over the past decade we have sampled menus at the majority of Vancouver's finest establishments, as well as the greasiest of greasy spoons. We are health conscious, in that we understand you need to eat to remain in good health.

In addition to brunch we are also passionate about coffee, eat, doughnuts and pastries

Our club has a modest origins, voracious appetites, and an appreciations for the brunching arts. If you would like to know more about the club, please see our about page.

If you would like to become a member of the club, please see the application page but please be aware that there are currently no vacancies in the roster.

VanCity Brunch Club is a private social club.
