Audience

The intended audience for this page is either a small company looking for copywriting services or for people who are looking for a chuckle. I decided to focus on the structure of the site while also providing some minor entertainment for myself. Hopefully someone else finds my sense of humour funny, too.

I chose a dark colour scheme with red text because dark websites are easier on the eyes in my opinon, and because it lent itself better to the ridiculous dinosaur graphics I had made. The red coloured courier font also looks like typewritten text using blood for ink, which makes sense if you are looking at the website for a pair of carnivore copywriters.

I think the red and black works well, but I will admit I am not 100% sure myself on the purple graphics. I think it works, but maybe not contrast-y enough.
