About Us

Tyrone O'Saurus

Tyrone was born in Alberta, Canada about 66 million years ago and after a simple upbringing as the king of all dinosaurs moved to Vancouver in 2004 to pursue a design degree at Emily Carr School University. Graduating at the top of his class he founded Jurassic Copywriting with his friend Raymond in 2009. He now lives in Mount Pleasant with his wife and three hatchlings.

Raymond Smith

Raymond was born in Mongolia 71 million years ago. He emigrated to Canada in 1992 after that countries peaceful revolution to pursue a better life, and a degree in creative writing from The University of British Columbia.

Raymond graduated in 1996 and worked for various creative firms around the city before meeting Tyrone at a meetup for dinosaur creatives. Their sensibilities matched up perfectly and they have been business partners ever since.
